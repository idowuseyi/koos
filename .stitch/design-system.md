## Elevation System
1. **Level 0 (Base):** Deep Slate (#0F172A) - infinite canvas
2. **Level 1 (Cards):** Charcoal (#1E293B) with 1px border (#334155)
3. **Level 2 (Modals):** Glassmorphic - 15% white tint, 20px backdrop blur, indigo-tinted shadow
4. **Interactive States:** Border-glow (Primary Indigo at 40% opacity) on hover

## Design Principles
- Aesthetic: Corporate Modern + Glassmorphism
- Glass surfaces: White or indigo at 4-8% opacity with 12-20px backdrop blur
- Gradients: Indigo to Violet (135deg) for "Intelligence in Motion"
- Functional colors: Success (Emerald 500), Warning (Amber 500), Error (Rose 500)
